Neutral Route

In the videogame Undertale, the Neutral Route is one of the three main routes that players can take. It is considered the default route and is achieved by playing the game without taking any actions that would push the player towards either the Pacifist or Genocide routes.


During the Neutral Route, the player's actions will determine the outcome of the game's story and which ending they will receive. The player will encounter various characters and monsters throughout the game and have the option to either spare or kill them. The choices the player makes will affect the relationships they build with these characters and ultimately determine the ending they receive.

If the player chooses to spare all of the major characters in the game and complete certain objectives, they will receive the Neutral Route's true ending. However, if the player kills any major characters or fails to complete certain objectives, they will receive one of several Neutral Route sub-endings.
